Input:

Ns:Number of spontaneous tracks
Ni: Number of induced tracks
ζ: The zeta calibration constant and its standard error
ρD: The induced track density in a co-irradiated dosimeter glass
spot size: The diameter of the LA-ICP-MS laser spot, in μm
A:Counting area, in μ2
U1, U2, ... : Uranium concentration (in ppm, for both the ζ-calibration and absolute methods), or U/Ca-ratios (for the ζ-calibration method only) measured by LA-ICP-MS
s[U1], s[U2], ... : The standard errors of U1, U2, ... . These should exclude any external sources of uncertainty for the calculation of radial plots or weighted means.
